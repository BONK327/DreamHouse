How we work

01

Consultation

We begin by discussing your needs, preferences, and budget, forming the concept of your future home.

02

Design

Our architects create a custom project with drawings and 3D visualizations. After approval, we prepare the necessary documents and estimates.

03

Construction

We start construction, ensuring quality and adherence to timelines at every stage. We keep you regularly informed about progress.

04

Project Handover

We conduct a final quality check, address any issues, and hand over the keys to your new home along with all documents and warranties.

FAQ

What is the process for designing a house?

Designing a house involves several key steps:

  • 1 Define Requirements

    Identify your needs, budget, and preferences.

  • 2 Site Analysis

    Evaluate the location, including zoning laws and environmental factors.

  • 3 Conceptual Design

    Create initial sketches and layouts.

  • 4 Detailed Plans

    Develop architectural drawings and specifications.

  • 5 Permits

    Obtain necessary approvals from local authorities.

  • 6 Construction

    Hire contractors and oversee the building process.

  • 7 Final Inspection

    Ensure everything meets standards before moving in.
